WHO WE ARE

Trusted Chartered Accountants in Kochi

Delivering reliable audit, tax, compliance, and advisory services aligned with regulatory standards and business objectives.

J KRISHNAN & CO is a Kochi-based Chartered Accountancy firm providing audit, taxation, accounting, compliance, and corporate advisory services to corporates, institutions, and individuals across India.

The firm is led by Nishanth Sebastian Jose, FCA, a qualified Chartered Accountant with extensive experience in statutory audits, taxation, corporate compliance, and advisory engagements. Our approach combines technical expertise with practical business insight.

We assist clients throughout the complete business lifecycle — from incorporation and statutory registrations to accounting, audits, restructuring, and strategic financial advisory.
