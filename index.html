ABOUT US

J KRISHNAN & CO

A professionally managed Chartered Accountancy firm delivering compliant, reliable, and value-driven financial solutions.

J KRISHNAN & CO is a Kochi-based Chartered Accountancy firm providing audit, taxation, accounting, compliance, and corporate advisory services to corporates, institutions, and individuals across India.

The firm is led by Nishanth Sebastian Jose, FCA, a qualified Chartered Accountant with extensive experience in statutory audits, taxation, corporate compliance, and advisory engagements. With a strong foundation in regulatory frameworks and industry best practices, we deliver practical and structured solutions aligned with our clients’ business objectives.

We support clients throughout the complete business lifecycle — from incorporation and statutory registrations to accounting, audits, restructuring, and strategic financial advisory. Our work is driven by professional integrity, technical excellence, and timely execution.

Audit & Assurance Services

Independent, objective, and compliance-focused audit solutions

Statutory Audits

Statutory audits conducted in accordance with applicable laws, accounting standards, and ICAI guidelines to ensure transparency and regulatory compliance.

Tax Audits

Comprehensive tax audits under the Income-tax Act to assess compliance, reporting accuracy, and statutory obligations.

Internal Audits

Risk-based internal audits designed to strengthen internal controls, governance frameworks, and operational efficiency.

Stock & Inventory Audits

Independent verification of inventory and stock records for banks, financial institutions, and management assurance.

Forensic Audits

Investigation-based audits to identify financial irregularities, fraud risks, and compliance breaches with detailed reporting.

Special Audits

Customized audit engagements tailored for specific regulatory, management, or stakeholder requirements.

Financial Audits

Independent examination of financial statements to ensure accuracy, fairness, and compliance with applicable accounting standards and statutory requirements.

Compliance Audits

Systematic review of regulatory, statutory, and internal policy compliance to mitigate risks and ensure adherence to legal obligations.
